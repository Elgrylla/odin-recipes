Burger

Description

With this recipe you will be able to make the best burgers in the world

They are going to be so massive then even the biggest animal on the planet will have no power after eating it.

Ingredients

  • Breat toast x2
  • Steaks x2
  • Salad
  • Tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • Burger Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook your steak 5mins
  2. Put the bread in your toaster 1mins
  3. Put cheese on the bread toast and put it in the oven 5mins
  4. Put salad and tomatoes on your bread
  5. Assemble the steak with the bread
  6. Finally, put the Burger sauce on the steak
